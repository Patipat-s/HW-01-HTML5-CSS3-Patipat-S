About us


20m31e is a worldwide leader in free-to-play online and mobile games. Founded in Thailand in 1994, 20m31e developed one of the world's first graphics-based massively multiplayer online games. 20m31e also pioneered the concept of microtransactions and the free-to-play business model, setting a new standard in which play is free, and users have the option to purchase in-game items to enhance their experience.
